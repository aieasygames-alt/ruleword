📖 About This Story

Ever wondered what it takes to run a political campaign? In Election Simulator, you're a community organizer running for mayor of Riverside City. Manage your campaign funds, ace televised debates, navigate unexpected scandals, and win the hearts and minds of voters. Every decision matters — from your debate strategy to how you handle a crisis. With AI-generated scenarios, every campaign is unique. Will you win in a landslide, or will this campaign spark something even bigger than an election?

🎮 How to Play

Read each campaign scenario and make strategic choices. Your decisions affect four stats: Approval (voter support), Funds (campaign budget), Momentum (media buzz), and Integrity (your moral standing). Balance your resources wisely and stay true to your values. The story has multiple endings based on your campaign strategy.

💡 Tips

  • • High Integrity can earn you key endorsements even with low Funds
  • • Don't ignore Scandal chapters — they can make or break your campaign
  • • Positive campaigns tend to yield better long-term results
  • • There are 4 possible endings — replay with different strategies
  • • The AI generates unique debate questions and events each time

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game based on real politics?

No, the setting and characters are fictional. However, the game explores real campaign dynamics like resource management, public relations, and voter engagement.

Can I win every time?

Not necessarily! Your choices matter, and the AI creates unpredictable scenarios. Sometimes losing can lead to the most interesting story endings.

How long is one playthrough?

A typical campaign takes 15-25 minutes to complete, depending on your reading speed and choices.
