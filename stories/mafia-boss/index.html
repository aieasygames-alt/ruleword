📖 About This Story

Step into the shoes of a rising star in a 1920s crime family during Prohibition. In this AI-powered strategy game, every negotiation matters. Navigate the complex web of five rival families, build alliances with unlikely partners, manage the loyalty of your crew, and keep the heat from law enforcement at bay. Will you become the city's next kingpin, a legendary diplomat, or go down in a blaze of glory? Powered by AI, every playthrough offers unique challenges, dialogue, and outcomes. Strategy and wit — not violence — are your weapons of choice.

🎮 How to Play

Read each story scene and make strategic choices when options appear. Your decisions affect four key stats: Loyalty (your crew's faith in you), Respect (how other families see you), Heat (law enforcement attention), and Wealth (your resources). Balance all four to succeed. Negotiate wisely — every word counts in this world. The story has multiple endings based on your overall strategy.

💡 Tips

  • • Keep your Heat low — too much attention from the law ends the game badly
  • • Balance Loyalty and Respect — you need both your crew and your rivals on your side
  • • Sometimes the best move is to walk away from a bad deal
  • • There are 4 possible endings — try different strategies to discover them all
  • • The AI creates unique scenarios each time, making every playthrough fresh
