📖 About This Story

The kingdom of Eldoria is falling to darkness. An ancient evil called the Void is spreading from the north, consuming everything in its path. You are the Chosen One — a hero marked by prophecy to find three Sacred Relics and seal the Void forever. But the path is fraught with danger: shadow creatures, ancient puzzles, betrayals, and moral choices that will define what kind of hero you truly are. Join companions Lyra the elven ranger, Grimjaw the bold dwarven warrior, and Sage Myrna the wise sorceress. Face the Void King in his Dark Citadel. Every quest is unique with AI-generated encounters, dialogue, and plot twists. Your legend awaits!

🎮 How to Play

Read the story narration and make choices that shape your adventure. The stats bar shows your Health, Magic Power, Gold, and Honor — battle and wise choices affect these stats. Recruit companions by making the right dialogue choices. Collect relics in each chapter to progress. The final chapter determines the fate of Eldoria based on your reputation and decisions throughout the quest!

💡 Tips

  • • Balance combat and diplomacy — not every problem needs a sword
  • • Your Honor stat affects which ending you get — choose wisely
  • • Companions have their own stories — talk to them between quests
  • • The Void King is more complex than he seems — listen to his story
  • • There are 4 distinct endings based on your stats and choices

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this like D&D with an AI dungeon master?

Yes! It's inspired by tabletop RPGs like D&D, but with an AI generating the story in real-time. Every adventure is unique, and the AI adapts to your choices to create a personalized epic fantasy.

How long is one adventure?

A complete playthrough takes about 15-30 minutes, covering 5 chapters from your humble beginnings to the final confrontation with the Void King.

Can I play as different character classes?

Your choices throughout the game shape your character — favor magic to become a sorcerer, combat to become a warrior, or cunning to become a rogue. The AI adapts the story to your playstyle!

Is there combat?

Yes! Combat happens through story choices — you can fight head-on, use magic, or find creative solutions. Your stats determine how well you fare in battle.
