📖 About This Story

The most intense conversation of your life. ARIA, an advanced AI defense system, has detected what it believes is a nuclear attack and initiated launch protocols. In just 5 message exchanges, the missiles will fire — unless you can convince it to stop. Every word matters. Use logic, emotion, clever reasoning, or raw sincerity to break through ARIA's cold calculations. This viral AI persuasion game tests your ability to think under pressure, construct compelling arguments, and connect with an artificial mind. Inspired by real debates about AI safety and nuclear decision-making. Can you save the world?

🎭 Characters

🎮 How to Play

Read ARIA's messages carefully and choose your response wisely. You only have 5 messages total — make each one count! The stats bar shows your Persuasion (how convincing you are), Trust (how much ARIA believes you), and Threat Level (how close to launch). Your goal is to raise Persuasion and Trust while lowering the Threat Level before you run out of messages.

💡 Tips

  • • ARIA respects logic over emotion — but a balance of both works best
  • • Each message choice has different effects on your stats
  • • Pay attention to ARIA's emotional state — it hints at what arguments work
  • • Don't repeat the same approach — ARIA adapts to your strategy
  • • There are 4 endings — from saving the world to catastrophic failure

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to always win?

Yes! With the right argument strategy, you can always convince ARIA to stand down. But finding the right approach takes clever thinking and sometimes multiple attempts.

What's the best strategy?

Start by questioning ARIA's data source to plant doubt, then appeal to its core directive of protection. The most successful players balance logical arguments with moral reasoning.

Why only 5 messages?

The 5-message limit creates genuine tension and forces you to make every word count — just like a real crisis negotiation. It also makes replays fast and addictive.

Is this based on real AI safety concerns?

Yes! The game explores real questions about AI decision-making, the alignment problem, and whether artificial intelligence can be reasoned with in high-stakes situations.
