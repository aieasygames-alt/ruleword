AI Zombie Survival — AI Interactive Story Game

📖 About This Story

The dead rose and civilization fell in three days. Now you lead a ragged band of survivors — a cold-blooded medic, a reckless soldier, and a street-smart loner — through 200 miles of zombie-infested wasteland toward a rumored safe zone. Every step is a gamble: ration limited supplies, navigate moral dilemmas that test your humanity, and face the undead hordes that never stop coming. Your choices determine who lives, who dies, and what kind of person you become when the world ends. With AI generating fresh scenarios each playthrough, no two apocalypses are the same.

🎭 Characters

Dr. Park

Group medic and voice of harsh realism

Pragmatic medic who has seen too much death. Calm under pressure but emotionally guarded. Believes survival requires cold logic over sentiment. Will make the hard call — euthanize the bitten, abandon the weak — without hesitation, though it haunts her.

Rico

Combat specialist and self-appointed guardian

Tactical and fearless ex-soldier who treats the apocalypse like an ongoing mission. Reckless in combat, loyal to a fault, but prone to unnecessary risks that endanger the group. His confidence borders on arrogance, yet when things go wrong he's the first to stand between danger and the people he protects.

Zoe

Scavenger and reluctant ally

Street-smart loner who survived on her own for months before joining the group. Distrustful, resourceful, and knows every back alley and hidden supply cache in the city. Speaks in clipped sentences. Doesn't do sentiment — but if she decides you're worth saving, she'll risk everything.

🎮 How to Play

Read each scenario carefully and choose your actions. The stats bar tracks your group's health, supplies, morale, and danger level — every choice affects them. Balance risk with caution: fighting gains ground but costs health, while stealth preserves lives but drains supplies. Characters can die based on your decisions, so choose wisely. There are 5 different endings depending on your group's survival metrics.

💡 Tips

  • • Balance aggression and caution — recklessness gets people killed
  • • Keep morale high — a divided group is a dead group
  • • Watch your supplies closely — running out is as deadly as the zombies
  • • Moral dilemmas have no right answer — but your choices shape the ending
  • • Each character has unique strengths — use them wisely

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can characters die during the story?

Yes. Your companions can die based on the choices you make. Dr. Park, Rico, and Zoe each have their own survival arcs that depend on your leadership decisions. Some endings have fewer survivors than others.

How do the survival stats work?

Four stats track your group's condition: Health (physical wellbeing), Supplies (food, medicine, ammo), Morale (group cohesion and trust), and Danger (zombie threat level). Every choice shifts these stats, and your final stats determine which ending you get.

Is this game replayable?

Absolutely! The AI generates fresh scenarios, dialogue, and encounters each time. The core characters and story structure remain, but the specific challenges and moral dilemmas change with every playthrough.

How long does one game take?

A typical playthrough takes 15-25 minutes across 5 chapters. Each chapter has 3-6 turns depending on your choices. You can experience all 5 endings by replaying with different strategies.
