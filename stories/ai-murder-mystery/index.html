📖 About This Story

Step into the shoes of a brilliant detective in our AI Murder Mystery! A powerful CEO has been found dead in his mansion during a stormy dinner party. Four suspects, each with secrets and motives, are waiting to be interrogated. Victor Ashford, the cold business rival. Elena Blackwell, the elegant young widow. Marcus Chen, the nervous tech genius. Diana Reeves, the sharp-tongued assistant who knows too much. Search the crime scene for clues, question suspects to expose contradictions, uncover hidden relationships, and build your case. With AI generating unique dialogue and plot twists every time, no two investigations are the same. Can you find the killer before dawn?

🎮 How to Play

Read the story and examine the crime scene. When you're ready, interrogate the suspects by choosing your questions carefully. Pay attention to contradictions in their stories and the evidence bar at the top. Each suspect has a suspicion meter — the higher it goes, the more likely they're guilty. Gather enough evidence to make your accusation in the final chapter!

💡 Tips

  • • Pay attention to contradictions between suspect alibis — someone is lying
  • • The evidence stat is crucial — search everywhere before making accusations
  • • Don't trust first impressions — the most nervous suspect isn't always guilty
  • • Ask about relationships between suspects — secrets connect them
  • • There are 4 possible endings depending on your detective skills

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the killer different each time?

Yes! The AI randomly determines the killer at the start of each game, so you can't just memorize the answer. Every playthrough is a fresh mystery.

How do I know who the killer is?

Pay close attention to alibi contradictions, physical evidence at the crime scene, and behavioral inconsistencies during interrogations. The clues are there — you just need to piece them together.

What happens if I accuse the wrong person?

If you make the wrong accusation, you'll get a different ending. The story adapts to your choice — sometimes new evidence surfaces at the last moment!

Can I replay the mystery?

Absolutely! Since the AI generates unique dialogue and the killer changes each time, every replay offers a completely new mystery to solve.
