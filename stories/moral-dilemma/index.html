Read each moral scenario carefully and choose the response that feels most true to who you are. There are no wrong answers. ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are there right or wrong answers?

No! Every answer is valid. The quiz maps your moral philosophy, not your morality. All perspectives are respected.

How many different results are there?

There are 6 moral profiles: The Idealist, The Pragmatist, The Guardian, The Philosopher, The Protector, and The Realist.

Can I retake the quiz?

Yes! Each playthrough generates unique scenarios, so you'll face different dilemmas each time.
