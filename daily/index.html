Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Daily Challenge?

Daily Challenges are special puzzle variants that change every day. Each game generates a unique puzzle based on the current date, so everyone gets the same challenge. It's a great way to test your skills against a fresh puzzle each day!

When does the daily puzzle reset?

Daily puzzles reset every night at midnight in your local timezone. After midnight, you'll get a brand new puzzle for each game. You can only play each day's puzzle once for a score.

Can I play old daily puzzles?

Currently, only today's daily puzzle is available. Each day brings a new challenge — miss a day and there's a fresh one waiting! This encourages daily engagement and keeps things exciting.

Are daily challenges free?

Absolutely! All daily challenges are completely free. No registration, no download, no payment required. Just click and play!
