## Tips for Outreach Success

### Before Sending:
- ✅ Research the site and personalize each email
- ✅ Find the right contact name (check About page, LinkedIn)
- ✅ Read their content and mention specifics
- ✅ Check if they have submission guidelines

### Email Best Practices:
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Personalize the first sentence
- Keep emails under 150 words
- Include clear call-to-action
- Use professional signature

### Follow-up Strategy:
- Wait 5-7 days before following up
- Maximum 2 follow-ups per prospect
- Change subject line slightly
- Offer value in follow-up (new content, data)

### Tracking:
- Use a spreadsheet or CRM
- Track: sent date, response, outcome
- Note what subject lines work best
- A/B test different approaches
