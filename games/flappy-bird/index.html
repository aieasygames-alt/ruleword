Flappy Bird Game Guide

📖 About

Flappy Bird is a simple yet addictive game where you tap to make the bird fly and navigate through gaps in pipes. Time your taps perfectly to maintain the right height and pass through the openings. One tap too many or too few means game over! How many pipes can you pass?

🎯 Objectives

Navigate through as many pipe gaps as possible without hitting them or the ground. Each successful pass scores one point. Challenge yourself to beat your high score!

🎮 How to Play

Tap the screen or press Space to make the bird jump. The bird falls due to gravity, so time your taps carefully to fly through the gaps in the pipes. Don't hit the pipes or fall to the ground!

⌨️ Controls

Click, tap, or press Space to make the bird jump. Hold nothing to let gravity pull the bird down.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Bird falls due to gravity
  • ▸ Each tap gives an upward boost
  • ▸ Pipes appear from the right
  • ▸ Pass through gaps to score
  • ▸ Hitting pipes or ground ends game

✨ Features

  • ★ Simple one-button gameplay
  • ★ Increasingly challenging gaps
  • ★ High score tracking
  • ★ Addictive retry mechanic
  • ★ Clean, minimal graphics

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Find a rhythm for tapping - don't spam
  • ✓ Stay in the middle of the screen when possible
  • ✓ Anticipate the gap position early
  • ✓ Stay calm - panic tapping leads to crashes

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Flappy Bird so hard?
The game requires precise timing and rhythm. The small hitbox and narrow gaps make it challenging, but that's what makes beating your high score so satisfying!
What's the best strategy?
Find a consistent tapping rhythm rather than reacting to each pipe. Stay in the middle third of the screen for maximum flexibility.
Does the game get faster?
The pipe speed remains constant, but concentration fatigue makes it feel harder over time!
