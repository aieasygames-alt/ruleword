Spelling Bee Game Guide

📖 About

Spelling Bee is the buzz-worthy word puzzle from The New York Times that has word lovers hooked! Given seven letters arranged in a honeycomb pattern, create as many valid words as possible. Every word must include the center letter, and words must be at least 4 letters long. Find the pangram (a word using all 7 letters) to earn Queen Bee status! This daily puzzle combines vocabulary challenge with the satisfaction of discovery.

🎯 Objectives

Find as many valid words as possible using the 7 honeycomb letters, always including the center letter. Discover the pangram (using all letters) for maximum points. Aim for 'Genius' or even 'Queen Bee' ranking by finding all possible words.

🎮 How to Play

Make words of 4+ letters using the honeycomb letters. Every word must contain the center letter. Pangrams (using all 7 letters) earn bonus points!

⌨️ Controls

Click or type letters to spell words. The center letter must be included in every word. Press Enter or click Submit to check your word.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Words must be at least 4 letters long
  • ▸ Every word must include the center letter
  • ▸ Letters can be used multiple times within a word
  • ▸ Pangram uses all 7 letters (huge bonus!)
  • ▸ Score ranks you from Beginner to Queen Bee

✨ Features

  • ★ Daily Puzzle - Same puzzle for everyone
  • ★ Progress Ranks - From Beginner to Queen Bee
  • ★ Pangram Hunt - Find the word using all letters
  • ★ Shuffle - Rearrange outer letters for new perspectives

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Always include the center letter
  • ✓ Look for plural forms of words
  • ✓ Find the pangram for big points
  • ✓ Common endings: -ING, -ED, -ER, -LY

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pangram?
A pangram is a word that uses all 7 available letters at least once. Finding the pangram gives you a big score boost and is often key to reaching higher ranks.
Can I use letters more than once?
Yes! You can use any letter multiple times in a word. For example, if you have 'L', you can spell 'LEVEL'.
How do I reach Queen Bee status?
Queen Bee requires finding all possible words in the puzzle. It's extremely difficult and often requires hours of play or collaborative effort.
What's the shuffle button for?
Shuffling rearranges the 6 outer letters randomly. This can help you see new letter combinations and break out of word-finding ruts.
