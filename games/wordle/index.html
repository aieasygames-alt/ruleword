Wordle Game Guide

📖 About

Wordle is the viral word puzzle phenomenon that took the world by storm! This elegantly simple yet fiendishly addictive game challenges you to guess a hidden 5-letter word in just six attempts. With its clever color-coded feedback system, each guess brings you closer to the solution while testing your vocabulary and deduction skills. The daily puzzle format creates a shared global experience, making Wordle the perfect morning brain teaser for millions of word game enthusiasts.

🎯 Objectives

Guess the secret 5-letter word within 6 attempts using the color-coded clues from each guess. Sharpen your vocabulary, pattern recognition, and logical deduction skills with this daily word puzzle challenge.

🎮 How to Play

Type a 5-letter word and press Enter. Green letters are correct in the right spot. Yellow letters are in the word but wrong position. Gray letters are not in the word.

⌨️ Controls

Use your keyboard to type a 5-letter English word, then press Enter to submit your guess. On mobile, tap the on-screen letters to form your word.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ You have exactly 6 attempts to guess the hidden 5-letter word
  • ▸ Green tiles indicate correct letters in the correct position
  • ▸ Yellow tiles indicate letters that exist in the word but in a different position
  • ▸ Gray tiles indicate letters not present in the word
  • ▸ Letters can appear multiple times in the word

✨ Features

  • ★ Daily Puzzle - Same word for everyone, updated daily
  • ★ Color-Coded Feedback - Visual clues guide your guesses
  • ★ Share Results - Share your success without spoilers
  • ★ Statistics - Track your win rate and streak

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Start with words containing common letters (R, S, T, L, N, E, A)
  • ✓ Use different letters each guess initially to maximize information
  • ✓ Yellow letters help narrow down possible positions
  • ✓ Think about common word patterns and letter combinations

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best starting word?
Many players use words like 'ADIEU', 'CRANE', 'SLATE', or 'RAISE' that contain common vowels and consonants to gather maximum information.
Can letters repeat in Wordle?
Yes! The hidden word can contain repeated letters. If your guess has a letter twice but the answer only has it once, only one will be colored.
When does the new puzzle appear?
A new Wordle puzzle is released every day at midnight local time, giving everyone the same daily challenge.
What if I can't solve it in 6 tries?
Don't worry! The game will reveal the answer after your 6th attempt. Use it as a learning opportunity for tomorrow's puzzle.
