Jewel Quest Game Guide

📖 About

Jewel Quest is the sparkling match-3 puzzle game that has captivated millions! Swap adjacent jewels to create lines of three or more matching gems. When matched, jewels disappear and new ones fall from above, potentially creating chain reactions and combos. With limited moves, strategic planning is essential to reach the target score. Dazzling gems, satisfying matches, endless fun!

🎯 Objectives

Reach the target score by matching jewels before running out of moves. Create matches of 3 or more by swapping adjacent gems. Plan combinations and cascades to maximize points. Longer matches and chain reactions score bonus points!

🎮 How to Play

Click a jewel, then click an adjacent jewel to swap them. Match 3 or more jewels of the same color horizontally or vertically to score points and clear them from the board.

⌨️ Controls

Click on a jewel to select it, then click on an adjacent jewel to swap their positions. If this creates a match of 3+ same-colored jewels, they disappear and new jewels fall from above.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I match 4 or more jewels?
Matching 4 or more jewels creates special power-ups in many versions. Even without special powers, longer matches score significantly more points. Always look for opportunities to create larger matches!
Why should I work from the bottom?
Matching jewels at the bottom causes more jewels to fall, increasing the chance of creating automatic cascades and combos. Bottom matches can trigger chain reactions across the entire board!
What are cascading combos?
When matched jewels disappear and new ones fall, they may automatically create new matches. These cascade combos multiply your score without using extra moves—highly valuable for reaching targets!
How many moves do I get?
Most levels give you 30 moves to reach the target score. Use them wisely—sometimes it's better to set up a big combo than to take an immediate small match.
