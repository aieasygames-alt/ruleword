Snake Game Guide

📖 About

Snake is the legendary mobile game that defined a generation! Guide a hungry serpent through a confined space, eating food pellets to grow longer with each meal. The challenge? Your own body becomes an obstacle as you grow! What starts as a simple game of eating and growing transforms into an intense navigation puzzle where one wrong move ends it all. This classic arcade experience tests your reflexes, planning, and spatial awareness!

🎯 Objectives

Guide your snake to eat as much food as possible and grow as long as you can. Avoid colliding with the walls or your own growing body. Achieve the highest score possible by surviving and growing your snake to epic lengths!

🎮 How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to control direction. Eat the food to grow longer and score points. The game ends if you hit a wall or your own body.

⌨️ Controls

Use Arrow Keys or WASD to change the snake's direction. The snake moves continuously in its current direction. You cannot reverse direction (go directly opposite).

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Snake grows longer when eating food
  • ▸ Speed increases as snake grows
  • ▸ Collision with walls ends game
  • ▸ Collision with own body ends game
  • ▸ Score based on length achieved

✨ Features

  • ★ Classic Gameplay - Authentic retro experience
  • ★ Progressive Difficulty - Gets harder as you grow
  • ★ Score Tracking - Beat your personal best
  • ★ Simple Controls - Easy to learn, hard to master

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Plan your path ahead, especially as you grow longer
  • ✓ Avoid getting trapped in your own coils
  • ✓ Stay near the center when possible for more escape routes
  • ✓ Use smooth, flowing movements rather than sharp turns

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long can a snake get?
In theory, a snake can fill the entire grid! However, as you grow longer, navigation becomes increasingly difficult. The challenge is managing your own body as an obstacle.
Why can't I reverse direction?
Reversing direction would cause an instant collision with your own body. The game prevents this to maintain fair play—you must plan turns in advance.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Follow a systematic pattern around the edges, leaving yourself an escape route. Many players use a zig-zag or spiral pattern to maximize space usage while avoiding self-collision.
Does the snake speed up?
Many versions increase speed as the snake grows longer, making the game progressively more challenging. This adds pressure to plan ahead rather than react.
