Police Escape Game Guide

📖 About

Police Escape is a turn-based chase puzzle. You play a thief who must draw a continuous escape route to the exit, while one or more police pursue via shortest-path AI. Plan your path, hit Go, and watch the chase play out — escape before they catch you! 30 hand-crafted levels introduce new mechanics every 5 stages: keys you must collect, teleport portals, ice blocks that block cells until they melt, and exits that toggle open and closed on a schedule. Play free online, no download.

🎯 Objectives

Draw a path from thief to exit that avoids the police. Collect all required keys. Arrive at the exit when it's open.

🎮 How to Play

Drag from the thief to the exit to draw your path. Press Go to commit. The thief and police advance one cell per turn; if a police catches you, you lose.

⌨️ Controls

Drag from 🥷 to 🚪 with mouse or touch. Backtrack by dragging onto the previous cell. Press Go! to commit the path.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do police move?
Each police computes the BFS shortest path to your CURRENT cell and steps one square along it. If they can't reach you (walled off), they walk toward their patrol point.
Why did I lose when our paths didn't overlap?
If the thief and a police trade cells in the same turn (thief moves into police's old spot while police moves into thief's old spot), that counts as a capture.
Are the levels solvable?
Yes. Every one of the 30 levels was verified solvable by a backtracking solver before shipping — there is always at least one winning path.
Does my progress save?
Yes, progress (unlocked levels and best step counts) is saved to your browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data resets it.
Can I replay earlier levels?
Yes. The level-select screen shows every unlocked level with your best step count, so you can revisit and improve.
