Space Invaders Game Guide

📖 About

Space Invaders is the 1978 arcade classic that revolutionized video games and launched the golden age of arcade gaming! Waves of alien invaders descend from above, moving left and right while gradually advancing toward Earth. Your mission: shoot them all before they reach the bottom. With its iconic pixel aliens, escalating difficulty, and satisfying shooter gameplay, Space Invaders remains the template for action games over 45 years later.

🎯 Objectives

Destroy all alien invaders before they reach the bottom of the screen. Earn points by shooting aliens—the bottom rows are worth more. Survive as many waves as possible to achieve the highest score.

🎮 How to Play

Use arrow keys to move left/right and Space to shoot. Destroy all aliens to advance. Aliens move faster as their numbers decrease. You have 3 lives.

⌨️ Controls

← → arrow keys move your ship left and right. Space bar fires your laser cannon. You can only have one shot on screen at a time.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ 11 columns × 5 rows of aliens descend together
  • ▸ Aliens move left/right, dropping down when hitting edges
  • ▸ Each destroyed alien is worth 10-40 points based on type
  • ▸ Aliens speed up as fewer remain
  • ▸ Mystery UFO occasionally flies overhead for bonus points

✨ Features

  • ★ Progressive Difficulty - Aliens speed up as you destroy them
  • ★ Protective Shields - Destructible barriers offer temporary cover
  • ★ Bonus UFO - Random high-value targets
  • ★ Lives System - 3 lives to beat your high score

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Stay directly under the invaders for easier shots
  • ✓ Shoot from edges when possible to reduce their spread
  • ✓ Watch for the speed increase as enemies decrease
  • ✓ Use predictable movement patterns to aim

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why do the aliens speed up?
As you destroy invaders, there are fewer to calculate positions for, so they move faster. This creates escalating tension as the level progresses.
What are the shields for?
The four shields provide temporary cover from alien fire. They gradually degrade as they're hit by both your shots and enemy fire.
How much is the UFO worth?
The mystery UFO that flies across the top is worth 50-300 points, randomly determined. It's a high-value bonus target.
What's the best strategy?
Focus on one column at a time from the edges inward. This limits how far the aliens can spread horizontally.
