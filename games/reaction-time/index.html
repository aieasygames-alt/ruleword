Reaction Speed Game Guide

📖 About

Reaction Speed is a precision timing test that measures how quickly you can respond to a visual stimulus. Wait for the screen to change from red to green, then click as fast as possible. Your reaction time is measured in milliseconds, giving you an accurate assessment of your reflexes. Track your average and best times across multiple attempts.

🎯 Objectives

React as quickly as possible when the green signal appears. Measure your reaction time in milliseconds. Track your average and best times. Compare your results with average human reaction times.

🎮 How to Play

Click to start the test. Wait for the red screen to turn green. Click as fast as you can when it turns green. Your reaction time will be displayed in milliseconds. Try multiple times to get your average.

⌨️ Controls

Click or tap the screen when the signal turns green. Wait patiently during the red phase.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ The signal changes from red to green after a random delay
  • ▸ Clicking too early (during red) resets the test
  • ▸ Your time is measured from the green signal to your click
  • ▸ Results are shown in milliseconds

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Stay focused and keep your finger ready on the mouse
  • ✓ Don't try to anticipate the green signal - wait for it
  • ✓ Do multiple attempts for a more accurate average
  • ✓ Average human reaction time is about 250ms

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good reaction time?
The average human reaction time is about 250ms. Below 200ms is considered fast, and below 150ms is exceptional.
Why did my test reset?
You clicked before the screen turned green. This counts as a false start and the test resets.
Can I improve my reaction time?
Yes! Regular practice, good sleep, and staying hydrated can help improve your reaction speed.
