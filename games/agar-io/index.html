Agar.io Game Guide

📖 About

Agar.io is a multiplayer-style survival game where you control a small cell in a petri dish world. Your goal is to eat smaller cells and food particles to grow larger while avoiding bigger cells that can eat you. The larger you grow, the slower you move, making strategic positioning crucial. Features AI opponents that hunt and flee based on size relationships.

🎯 Objectives

Eat smaller cells and food to grow your cell size. Avoid larger cells that can consume you. Grow as large as possible to dominate the arena. Use speed and positioning strategically to survive.

🎮 How to Play

Move your mouse or touch the screen to direct your cell. Your cell follows your cursor/finger automatically. Eat cells smaller than you to grow in size. Run away from cells larger than you. The larger you are, the slower you move.

⌨️ Controls

Move your mouse or touch the screen to direct your cell. Your cell follows your cursor automatically.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ You can only eat cells significantly smaller than you (at least 10% smaller)
  • ▸ When you eat a cell, you grow by absorbing part of its mass
  • ▸ Larger cells move slower than smaller cells
  • ▸ AI opponents will chase smaller cells and flee from larger ones

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Stay near the edges early on to avoid larger predators
  • ✓ Collect food particles to grow steadily before hunting other cells
  • ✓ Use your speed advantage when small to escape danger
  • ✓ Watch for AI cells fighting each other and steal the remains

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I eat cells that are only slightly smaller?
You need to be at least 10% larger than another cell to consume it. This prevents unfair instant kills.
How do I get a high score?
Grow as large as possible by eating many cells. Your score is based on your final cell size.
Do larger cells have any disadvantages?
Yes, larger cells move slower, making it harder to chase prey or escape from even larger threats.
