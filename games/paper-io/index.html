Paper.io Game Guide

📖 About

Paper.io is a territory capture game where you move your character around a grid to create trails. When you return to your territory, the enclosed area becomes yours. Watch out for other players who can cut your trail - if you hit your trail or an enemy trail, game over! Features AI opponents that compete for territory.

🎯 Objectives

Capture as much territory as possible. Return to your territory to enclose captured areas. Avoid hitting your own trail or enemy trails. Eliminate AI opponents by cutting their trails.

🎮 How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Move outside your territory to create a trail. Return to your territory to enclose the captured area. Don't hit your own trail or game over! Cut enemy trails to eliminate them.

⌨️ Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to move. Your character moves in four directions on the grid.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ You must be in your territory to create a trail
  • ▸ Enclosed areas are filled when you return to territory
  • ▸ Hitting any trail (yours or enemy) ends the game
  • ▸ Captured territory from eliminated players transfers to you

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Start by expanding in safe directions near your territory
  • ✓ Don't make your trail too long - return frequently
  • ✓ Watch for AI players and cut their trails when they extend
  • ✓ Claim neutral territory between you and your base

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I capture territory?
Move outside your territory, create a trail, then return to your territory to enclose the area.
What happens if I hit my trail?
Game over. Be careful not to cross your own trail or hit enemy trails.
Can I eliminate AI opponents?
Yes! Cross their trail to eliminate them, and their territory becomes yours.
