Mahjong Solitaire Game Guide

📖 About

Mahjong Solitaire is a relaxing tile-matching puzzle that combines the beauty of traditional Chinese mahjong with engaging solitaire gameplay! Your goal is to clear a beautifully arranged stack of tiles by matching identical pairs. But there's a catch—only 'free' tiles can be matched, meaning they have at least one side (left or right) unblocked. With multiple layouts and soothing gameplay, Mahjong Solitaire offers the perfect escape for puzzle lovers seeking a calm yet challenging experience.

🎯 Objectives

Clear the entire board by matching pairs of identical free tiles. A tile is free if at least one of its long sides (left or right) is not blocked by another tile. Complete the puzzle as quickly as possible while enjoying the meditative matching process.

🎮 How to Play

Click on two matching tiles that are 'free' (at least one left or right side is open, and no tile is on top). The tiles will be removed. Continue until all tiles are cleared or no more moves are possible.

⌨️ Controls

Click on a free tile to select it, then click on a matching free tile to remove the pair. Free tiles are highlighted when selected.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Tiles must be identical to be matched (same symbol/pattern)
  • ▸ Only 'free' tiles can be selected - at least one side unblocked
  • ▸ Tiles on top block tiles below from being selected
  • ▸ Matching removes both tiles from the board
  • ▸ Game ends when board is cleared or no valid moves remain

✨ Features

  • ★ Multiple Layouts - Various classic arrangements
  • ★ Hint System - Find available matches
  • ★ Shuffle - Rearrange tiles when stuck
  • ★ Timer - Challenge yourself for speed

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Focus on removing tiles from the top layers first to free more tiles
  • ✓ Plan ahead - some matches may block future moves
  • ✓ Look for tiles that appear 3 or 4 times - matching them opens more options
  • ✓ Use hints sparingly to develop pattern recognition skills

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a tile 'free'?
A tile is free when at least one of its long sides (left or right) is completely unblocked AND no tile is sitting on top of it. Only free tiles can be matched.
Can all Mahjong Solitaire games be won?
Not always. Some tile arrangements may become unwinnable if you run out of valid matches. Use hints to check if moves are still available.
What's the best strategy?
Remove tiles from higher layers first to expose more options. Avoid matching tiles that would trap identical tiles underneath.
Why can't I match these identical tiles?
Even if tiles look identical, they may not both be 'free'. Check if either tile is blocked on both sides or has a tile on top of it.
