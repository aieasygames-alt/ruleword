Match-3 Game Guide

📖 About

Match-3 is the addictive puzzle mechanic that has captivated casual gamers worldwide! Swap adjacent gems to create lines of 3 or more matching colors. Matched gems disappear in a satisfying cascade, with new gems falling from above. Create combos and chain reactions for massive bonus points. Simple to start, endlessly engaging—this is the definitive casual puzzle experience!

🎯 Objectives

Reach the target score before running out of moves by matching gems. Create longer matches (4 or 5 gems) for special power-ups. Chain combos together for maximum points and spectacular cascades!

🎮 How to Play

Click on a gem, then click an adjacent gem to swap them. Match 3 or more gems of the same color to remove them. New gems fall from above. Reach the target score before running out of moves.

⌨️ Controls

Click on a gem to select it, then click an adjacent gem to swap their positions. Only swaps that create a match of 3+ are allowed. Drag-and-drop also works.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Match 3+ gems of the same color in a row or column
  • ▸ Matched gems disappear and new gems fall from above
  • ▸ Match 4 gems to create a special gem (clears row/column)
  • ▸ Match 5 gems for a powerful rainbow gem
  • ▸ Combos: Chain reactions score bonus multipliers

✨ Features

  • ★ Special Gems - Created by matching 4+ gems
  • ★ Cascade Combos - Chain reactions for bonus points
  • ★ Move Limit - Strategic resource management
  • ★ Target Score - Clear goals to achieve

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Look for matches at the bottom to trigger chain reactions
  • ✓ Plan moves that create multiple matches simultaneously
  • ✓ Save special gems for when you really need them
  • ✓ Focus on completing objectives over high scores

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What do special gems do?
Match 4 gems creates a line-clearing gem. Match 5 creates a rainbow gem that clears all gems of one color. Special combinations have unique effects!
How do combos work?
When gems fall and create new matches automatically, each consecutive match in the chain adds a score multiplier. Longer chains = bigger bonuses!
What's the best strategy?
Focus on creating special gems rather than basic 3-matches. Look for opportunities to set up chain reactions. Bottom-of-board matches often trigger cascades.
Can I swap any two gems?
Only adjacent gems can be swapped, and the swap must create at least one match of 3 or more. Invalid swaps are rejected.
