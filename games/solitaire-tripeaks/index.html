Solitaire TriPeaks Game Guide

📖 About

Solitaire TriPeaks is an addictive card game that combines strategy with luck! Three pyramids of cards await—you must clear them by playing cards that are one rank higher or lower than the current waste pile card. Unlike classic solitaire, suit doesn't matter here! Plan your moves to uncover buried cards, create long chains for bonus points, and clear all three peaks to win!

🎯 Objectives

Remove all cards from the three pyramid peaks by playing sequentially—each card played must be one rank higher or lower than the top waste card. Clear the entire board before running out of stock cards!

🎮 How to Play

Click the stock to draw cards. Play cards from the peaks that are exactly one rank higher than the top waste card (any suit). Clear all cards to win!

⌨️ Controls

Click on face-up cards from the peaks to play them onto the waste pile. Click the stock pile to draw a new card when no moves are available.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play cards of any suit?
Yes! In TriPeaks, suit doesn't matter. You can play any card that's one rank higher or lower than the waste pile, regardless of suit color or symbol.
What happens when I run out of moves?
Click the stock pile to draw a new card. If the stock runs out, you can cycle through it—but recycling usually costs points!
Should I clear one peak first or balance them?
Generally, try to keep options open by playing from multiple peaks. However, clearing one peak early can be strategic if it unlocks many buried cards.
What's a streak bonus?
Playing consecutive cards without drawing from stock builds a streak. Longer streaks multiply your points—plan ahead to maximize chains!
