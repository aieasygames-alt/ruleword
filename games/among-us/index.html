Among Us Game Guide

📖 About

Experience the fun of Among Us in this arcade tribute! Control your character as it moves through the spaceship, using the kill button to eliminate targets and sabotage to create chaos. While simplified from the full social deduction game, this captures the spirit and characters that made Among Us a global phenomenon. Move smoothly, time your actions, and enjoy the iconic crewmate animations!

🎯 Objectives

Navigate your character around the map, eliminate targets when they're nearby, and trigger sabotage effects. In exploration mode, simply enjoy moving your character around and watching the animations.

🎮 How to Play

Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move your character around. Click the Kill button to eliminate nearby targets. Click the Sabotage button to trigger chaos effects. In Character Move mode, simply explore and enjoy the animations!

⌨️ Controls

Arrow Keys or WASD move your character in all directions. Click on-screen buttons for Kill and Sabotage actions. Movement is smooth and responsive.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the full Among Us game?
No, this is a simplified arcade tribute featuring the iconic character and some signature moves. It captures the spirit of Among Us in a casual arcade format.

What does sabotage do?
Sabotage triggers visual effects that simulate the chaos from the real game—flashing lights, alarms, and other distractions.
Can I play with others?
