Block Puzzle Game Guide

📖 About

Block Puzzle is the addictive puzzle game that combines Tetris shapes with Sudoku-style grid clearing! Place various shaped blocks onto the grid, trying to complete full rows or columns. When a line is complete, it clears and scores points. With no time pressure, you can carefully plan each placement—but the game ends when you can't fit any remaining pieces!

🎯 Objectives

Place all available blocks onto the grid to score points. Complete horizontal or vertical lines to clear them and earn bonus points. Maximize your score by clearing multiple lines at once. The game ends when no remaining pieces can fit!

🎮 How to Play

Click a shape button below the grid, then click on the grid to place it. Complete horizontal or vertical lines to clear them. Game ends when you can't place any more pieces!

⌨️ Controls

Select a piece from the available options, then click on the grid where you want to place it. Pieces cannot overlap or extend beyond the grid. Plan ahead to maximize line clears!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best strategy for high scores?
Focus on keeping the board open by clearing lines regularly. Avoid creating isolated single cells that can only be filled by 1×1 pieces. Try to set up multiple line clears for bonus points.
Should I clear lines immediately or wait?
It depends! Clearing lines immediately keeps the board open, but waiting can let you set up double or triple line clears for more points. Balance both strategies based on available pieces.
What happens when I can't place a piece?
You can skip that piece and try others. However, the game ends when NONE of the available pieces can be placed anywhere on the grid. Keep your board open!
Is this like Tetris?
Similar shapes, but different gameplay! Unlike Tetris, pieces don't fall—you place them strategically. There's no time pressure, and you can see all available pieces before deciding. It's more like a puzzle than an action game.
