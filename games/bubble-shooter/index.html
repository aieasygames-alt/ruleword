Bubble Shooter Game Guide

📖 About

Bubble Shooter is the addictive puzzle game that has captivated millions! A cluster of colorful bubbles descends from the top of the screen, and you must shoot matching bubbles to create groups of three or more. When bubbles connect, they pop and disappear! Clear all bubbles before they reach the bottom line, and use clever bank shots off walls to hit tricky angles. Simple mechanics, endless satisfaction!

🎯 Objectives

Clear all bubbles from the screen by matching three or more of the same color. Aim and shoot your bubble launcher to create matching groups. Prevent bubbles from reaching the bottom danger line or the game ends!

🎮 How to Play

Aim and click to shoot bubbles upward. Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to pop them. Clear all bubbles to win. Don't let bubbles reach the bottom line!

⌨️ Controls

Move your mouse to aim the bubble launcher. The dotted line shows your trajectory. Click to shoot the bubble. You can bounce shots off the side walls for angled positions.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when bubbles disconnect from the top?
Any bubbles that lose their connection to the top of the screen fall down and give you bonus points! This is a key strategy—cutting the connection of large clusters to clear them all at once.
How do wall bounces work?
Your bubbles can bounce off the side walls, changing their angle. Use this to reach bubbles that can't be hit with a straight shot. The trajectory preview helps you aim bank shots.
Do new bubbles keep appearing?
In many versions, a new row of bubbles pushes down from the top periodically or after a certain number of shots. This adds pressure to clear bubbles quickly before they reach the danger line.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Focus on creating large disconnections to drop massive clusters. Look for bubbles that are holding up many others below them—popping these can create huge cascades of falling bubbles for big bonus points.
