Text Twist Game Guide

📖 About

Text Twist is the fast-paced word game that challenges your vocabulary under pressure! Given six scrambled letters, you must form as many valid words as possible before the timer runs out. Find at least one 6-letter word to advance to the next round. The combination of time pressure and word-finding makes this an addictive brain workout for language lovers!

🎯 Objectives

Form as many words as possible from the given letters within the time limit. You must find at least one word using all letters (typically 6 letters) to advance to the next round. Score points based on word length and clear all words to get bonus points!

🎮 How to Play

Click letters to add them to your word, then click Submit to check if it's valid. You can also click Enter to submit. Find as many words as you can within 2 minutes!

⌨️ Controls

Click on letters to build your word, then click Submit or press Enter. You can also use keyboard shortcuts. Click Shuffle to rearrange letters and see new possibilities.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • ▸ Use the given letters to form words
  • ▸ Words must be 3+ letters long
  • ▸ Find the 6-letter word to advance
  • ▸ Timer counts down continuously
  • ▸ Clear all words for bonus points

✨ Features

  • ★ Time Challenge - 2-minute rounds
  • ★ Shuffle Button - Rearrange letters
  • ★ Word Counter - Track your progress
  • ★ Bonus Points - Find all words

💡 Tips

  • ✓ Look for common prefixes and suffixes
  • ✓ Try different letter combinations
  • ✓ Longer words give more points
  • ✓ Use Skip if you're stuck (costs 50 points)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I advance to the next round?
You must find at least one word that uses all six letters. Without the 6-letter word, you cannot advance even if you found all other words. Prioritize finding the full-length word!
What's the minimum word length?
Words must be at least 3 letters long. Two-letter combinations don't count as valid words in Text Twist, so focus on finding 3, 4, 5, and 6 letter words.
Should I use the shuffle button?
Yes! Shuffling rearranges the letters visually, which can help you see combinations you missed. It's free to use and often reveals new word possibilities.
What happens if I find all words?
Congratulations! You'll receive bonus points and advance to the next round. This is the ideal outcome, though it requires extensive vocabulary and quick thinking.
