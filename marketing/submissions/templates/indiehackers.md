## 标题

I built RuleWord - 63 free online puzzle games, no downloads required. Here's my story.

## 正文

Hey IndieHackers! 👋

After months of work, I'm excited to share RuleWord - a free online puzzle game platform.

## The Problem

I love classic puzzle games like Wordle, Sudoku, and Tetris. But I got frustrated with:

- Game sites cluttered with ads
- Required app downloads
- Time limits and paywalls
- Poor mobile experience

## The Solution

I built RuleWord - a clean, simple platform with 63+ classic games:

- Zero ads
- No downloads or registration
- Works on all devices
- 8 languages supported

## Games Included

**Word Games (8):** Wordle, Crosswordle, Hangman, Spelling Bee, Connections, Word Search, Boggle, Anagrams

**Logic Puzzles (20+):** Sudoku (6 variants), 2048, Minesweeper, Nonogram, Kakuro, KenKen, Nurikabe, Star Battle, Slitherlink, and more

**Board Games (7):** Chess, Checkers, Gomoku, Connect Four, Reversi, Chinese Chess, Battleship

**Arcade Classics (10):** Tetris, Pac-Man, Space Invaders, Snake, Frogger, Pong, Breakout, Asteroids

**Memory Games (6):** Simon, Memory, Pattern Memory, Number Memory, Reaction Test

## Stats

- 63 games live
- 8 languages
- 100% free forever
- Mobile-responsive

## What's Next

- Adding more games weekly
- User accounts (optional) for progress tracking
- Daily challenges
- Leaderboards
