## 标题

Show HN: RuleWord – 63 free online puzzle games, no ads, no downloads

## 正文

Hi HN! I built RuleWord, a free online puzzle game platform with 63+ classic games.

Why I built this:
- I love puzzle games but got tired of sites with too many ads
- Most game sites require downloads or have time limits
- I wanted something clean and simple

Features:
- 63+ games (Wordle, Sudoku, Tetris, Chess, Pac-Man, 2048, etc.)
- No ads, no registration, no downloads
- Works on all devices
- 8 languages

Games are organized into:
- Word games (Wordle, Crosswordle, Hangman, etc.)
- Logic puzzles (Sudoku variants, 2048, Minesweeper, etc.)
- Board games (Chess, Checkers, Gomoku, etc.)
- Arcade classics (Tetris, Pac-Man, Space Invaders, etc.)
- Memory games (Simon, Pattern Memory, etc.)

Tech stack: Astro + React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS

Link: https://ruleword.com/

Happy to answer questions about the tech or the games!
